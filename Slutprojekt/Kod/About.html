Who are we?

We are the company that created the world famous energy drink Red Bull, which is the most sold of all the energy drinks! We don't just sell drinks of course, we are also sponsoring many of the extreme sports all over the world, such as Parkour, skateboarding, skiing and many more! Red Bull came to exist in 1987, in Austria. Through out these 30 years Red Bull has existed, it has grown very rapidly and we are now one of the most well known sports companies!
