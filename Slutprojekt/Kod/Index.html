Red Bull Art of Motion


The ultimate freerunning event, Red Bull Art of Motion, returned to Santorini on October 1st, 2016, for the 6th consecutive year! And it will be back now in 2017! There are two ways you can take part in this year’s main competition: The first is through a video application via the Online Qualifier, where you have to make a 90 second video showing your skills in the sport. The judges will then decide if you are capable enough to take part in the main event. The second is through the Onsite Qualifier, where you fly to Santorini, and take part in a small competition with others. The three best athletes will be able to take part in the main event.
